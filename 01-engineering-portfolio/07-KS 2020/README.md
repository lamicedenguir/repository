In 2020, Lamice Denguir founded the startup Knowledge & Skills SAS in Macon France
and was its CEO between 2020 and 2022. 

In this folder you will find the files:
 - Founding the startup: you will find inside the fundamental deliverables that allowed
 the seeding of the startup and the first round of funding: the business plan, the 
 business model canvas, the commercial strategy, and the pitch deck.
 - Products demos: Knowledge & Skills produced 3 distinct platforms: KS-Portal, KS-Journal
 and KS-Conference. In the corresponding folders, you will find videos and images of 
 those platforms.
 - Social media videos: Dr. Denguir produced several promotional audiovisual material
 for the platforms. This folder cotains examples. 

The platform KS-Portal was candidate to several contests and grants applications. 
It earned the AWS EdStart Innovator label, the AWS Activate grant, France Active Bourgogne
and BPI grant, and was pre-qualified for LaFrenchTech Tremplin.
