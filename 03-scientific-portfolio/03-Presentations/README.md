This folder contains presentations in seminars and conferences performed by Dr. Denguir. 
Here is te list of what you should find:
  
  - 2013-Manufacturing21: This presentation is entitled "CARACTERISATION ET MODELISATION DE L’ETAT MICROSTRUCTURAL ET 
  MECANIQUE DES SOUS-COUCHES AFFECTEES PAR LE TOURNAGE DE SUPERFINITION DU CUIVRE Cu-c2 ET IMPACT SUR LA RESISTANCE A 
  LA CORROSION" and presented on October 9th 2013 during Manufacturing 21 seminar in Angers, France.
  
  - 2014-CIRP CSI: This presentation is entitled "INFLUENCE OF CUTTING PROCESS MECHANICS ON SURFACE INTEGRITY AND 
  ELECTROCHEMICAL BEHAVIOR OF OFHC COPPER" and presented on May 29th 2014 during the 2nd CIRP CSI conference in Nottingham, 
  United Kingdom.
  
  - 2014-JDD-CEA: This presentation is entitled "CARACTERISATION ET MODELISATION DE L’ETAT MICROSTRUCTURAL ET

  - 2015-CIRP-GA: This presentation is entitled "Experimental and numerical assessment of subsurface plastic deformation

  - 2015-ForumENIM: This presentation is entitled "Vers une meilleure caractérisation de l'intégrité de surface induite

  - 2016-CIRP-HPC: This presentation is entitled "Orthogonal cutting simulation of OFHC copper using a new constitutive model

  - 2016-CIRPWinterMeeting: This presentation is entitled "Physical based constitutive model for surface integrity prediction

  - 2016-JDD-CEA: This presentation is entitled "CARACTERISATION ET MODELISATION DE L’ETAT MICROSTRUCTURAL ET

  - 2017-CIRPWinterMeeting: This presentation is entitled "On the relationship between the cutting process, surface
