# 🎓 KS-Portal

> **A peer-reviewed learning platform connecting engineers, researchers, and scientific educators worldwide.**

KS-Portal is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed to modernize the dissemination of engineering and scientific knowledge through peer-reviewed educational video content, virtual classrooms, and conference replay services.

Originally developed under the **Knowledge & Skills** startup, the platform combines the credibility of traditional scientific publishing with the accessibility of modern e-learning technologies.

---

## Vision

Scientific knowledge has traditionally been communicated through books, journals, and conference proceedings.

KS-Portal was created from a simple question:

> **Why shouldn't educational videos undergo the same quality assurance process as scientific publications?**

The project introduces the concept of **peer-reviewed audiovisual scientific content**, enabling engineers and researchers to publish trusted educational videos evaluated by an international editorial committee before publication.

The objective is to make technical knowledge:

- more accessible,
- easier to understand,
- available worldwide,
- while maintaining scientific rigor.

---

# The Problem

Engineers and scientists often face two major challenges.

## Learners

Professionals and graduate students spend considerable time searching for reliable technical information scattered across books, research papers, and the Internet.

Finding:

- trustworthy content,
- qualified instructors,
- and specialized training

can be both difficult and time-consuming.

---

## Content Creators

Experts, professors, and researchers possess valuable knowledge but often lack:

- multimedia production skills,
- marketing resources,
- an online learning platform,
- technical infrastructure,
- international visibility.

KS-Portal was designed to bridge this gap.

---

# Solution

KS-Portal provides an integrated ecosystem where engineers, researchers, universities, and companies can create, review, distribute, and access high-quality engineering education.

The platform combines:

- 📚 Online engineering courses
- 🎥 Scientific video tutorials
- 🧪 Peer-review workflow
- 🎓 Virtual classrooms
- 📺 Scientific conference replay
- 💳 Secure content marketplace
- 👤 Role-based user management

---

# Main Features

## 🎓 Learning Portal

Users can:

- browse engineering courses
- purchase educational content
- watch technical tutorials
- access conference recordings

---

## 👨‍🏫 Virtual Classroom

Experts can organize live online sessions with participants worldwide.

---

## 🧪 Peer Review System

One of the platform's most distinctive features.

Educational content is reviewed by qualified experts before publication, improving technical quality and reliability.

The editorial committee originally included **20 professors, senior engineers, and researchers** representing institutions across Europe, North America, the Middle East, South America, and Asia.

---

## 📺 Conference Replay

Conference organizers can publish recorded scientific presentations securely for registered participants.

This capability later evolved into the standalone **KS-Conference** project.

---

## 💼 Marketplace

The platform allows instructors to monetize their expertise through:

- recorded courses
- virtual classes
- educational resources

while learners purchase only the content they need.

- Build technical skills

---

### Talent

- Submit educational videos

- Generate revenue from course sales

### Editorial Committee

- Review submitted content
- Ensure scientific quality

# Technologies

The original platform architecture included:

- PHP
- JavaScript
- HTML
- CSS
- MySQL

Cloud Infrastructure

- Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- AWS Activate for Startups
- AWS EdStart

# Design Process

The complete user experience was first designed in **Adobe XD** before implementation.

The project included:

- marketing website
- SaaS platform
- administrator dashboard
- instructor interface
- learner portal
- editorial workflow
- payment integration

The complete product was designed and brought to production in approximately **nine months**.

# Business Model

KS-Portal adopted a marketplace approach.

### Learners

Purchase:

- online courses
- virtual classrooms
- conference replay access

### Instructors

Earn revenue by selling:

- recorded courses
- live training sessions

Additional premium services included:

- multimedia production assistance
- educational content editing
- peer-review services
- promotional support
