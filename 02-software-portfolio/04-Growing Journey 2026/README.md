# 🌱 Growing Journey - iOS and Android App

> **A privacy-first digital journal that helps parents preserve the memories, milestones, and everyday moments of childhood.**

Growing Journey is a mobile application designed to help parents document their child's development in a secure and organized way.

From first words and first steps to school achievements and family adventures, Growing Journey provides a private space where memories can be preserved through journal entries, photos, videos, audio recordings, and important documents.

Unlike many family applications, **Growing Journey keeps your data entirely on your own device**. No accounts, no cloud storage, no advertising, and no tracking.

---

# Inspiration

As a parent, I realized that childhood is made up of thousands of little moments that quickly fade with time.

While smartphones make it easy to take pictures, those memories often become scattered across photo galleries, messaging apps, and paper documents.

Growing Journey was created to bring everything together into a single, organized timeline dedicated to one child, allowing parents to preserve both everyday memories and important milestones.

# Privacy First

Privacy was a fundamental design principle from the beginning.

Growing Journey intentionally avoids collecting personal information.

The application:

- ✅ stores all data locally on the device
- ✅ does not require user accounts
- ✅ does not use analytics
- ✅ does not display advertisements
- ✅ does not track users
- ✅ does not upload memories to external servers

Parents remain in complete control of their family's memories.

---

# Design Philosophy

Growing Journey was designed around three core principles:

### Simplicity

The interface focuses on writing and preserving memories rather than managing complicated settings.

### Privacy

Personal family memories should belong only to the family.

### Longevity

A childhood journal should remain accessible for years without depending on online services or subscriptions.
