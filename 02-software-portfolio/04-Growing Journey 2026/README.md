                 Growing Journey - Child Journal
                       
-----------------------------------------------------------------

Privacy-first cross-platform digital journal helping parents document and analyze child development while keeping all data locally stored.

Capture the moments that matter 🌱
---------------------------------

Growing Journey is a private journaling app designed to help parents document a child’s development, memories, and milestones in one secure place.
From first words to school achievements, Growing Journey helps you create a personal record of your child's journey through photos, notes, and memories.
All your memories stay safe, private, and stored directly on your device.

Key Features
------------

📔 Personal Journal
Write daily or occasional entries to document meaningful moments in your child's life.
📅 Calendar Notes
Quickly add notes to specific dates to track milestones, activities, or special events.
📷 Photos, Videos, and Audio
Attach media to journal entries to preserve memories exactly as they happened.
📄 Document Storage
Store PDF files and important documents related to your child’s development.
🔒 Private and Secure
Your journal is protected with a 4-digit PIN, ensuring that only you can access your memories.
📱 Designed for iPhone and iPad
Growing Journey adapts beautifully to both iPhone and iPad screens for a comfortable writing experience.

Your Data Stays Private
-----------------------

Growing Journey is designed with privacy in mind.
The app:
• does not collect personal data 
• does not use analytics or tracking 
• does not send information to servers 
• stores all data locally on your device
Your memories remain completely under your control.
